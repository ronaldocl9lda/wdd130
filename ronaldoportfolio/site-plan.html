Home page

About me. My name is Ronaldo Campos Lucas, I was born and live in Costa Rica. I come from a family of six people, my parents and my three older brothers. I graduated from a professional technical college in Costa Rica where I acquired knowledge in the area of information technology. I am currently studying at BYU Idaho University where I am following my path to become a Full-Stack developer. Web development is one of my passions, I want to grow professionally in this field, I seek to develop my skills through online courses and projects. I made this portfolio with the aim of showing everyone my progress and my skills and my willingness to bring all this knowledge to the workplace, I am a person who loves challenges.

Projects

3caras. This project is an email template made with HTML and CSS in which different styles were used to give the template creativity. 3caras is the name of an entertainment business and this template will help them send different emails to their specific audience, in such a way that they receive different communications in a very attractive email to see and not ignore.

Hangman. Hangman is a game in which people have to guess a word and have a certain number of guesses. I did this project using the programming language known as Python, where I had to use important concepts such as functions and object-oriented programming.

Courses

HTML. Through the platform known as Udemy, I was able to take Professor Ubaldo Acosta's complete HTML course. I was able to learn the most important concepts of HTML and I was able to put it into practice to create the skeletons of my projects.
